This vinyl setlist is a deep dive into the psychedelic and borderless soundscapes of cumbia, surf rock, and global grooves. Opening with Talking Heads' "Listening Wind", the selection sets a tone of atmospheric storytelling, where hypnotic rhythms and layered instrumentation guide the listener through a sonic journey of displacement, mysticism, and resistance. From there, the set moves seamlessly into the raw, electric energy of Afrosound and Chicha Libre, where Peruvian chicha and Colombian cumbia blend with surf rock influences, creating a hypnotic pulse that bridges past and present. Los Wembler's De Iquitos' "La Danza del Petrolero" stands as a ritualistic ode to the Amazon, while Juaneco Y Su Combo's tracks explore both the celebration and melancholy of the jungle's deep-rooted traditions.

The latter half of the setlist expands even further, embracing playful and experimental takes on cumbia and tropical psychedelia. Pancho Acosta’s “Egyptian Reggae” introduces a fusion of reggae grooves with South American textures, while "Cumbia Sobre el Mar" by Cumbia en Moog reimagines cumbia with cosmic synth explorations. Los Destellos and Los Orientales de Paramonga bring a raw, guitar-driven energy, fusing tropical melodies with fuzzed-out distortion, reminiscent of classic chicha. The closing selections, like "Noche Serena" by Ritmos Tropicosmos, drift into more meditative and ethereal territory, leaving listeners suspended in a trance-like groove. This set is an exploration of cumbia’s adaptability—a journey through deep rhythms, psychedelic textures, and the ever-evolving language of global sound.
