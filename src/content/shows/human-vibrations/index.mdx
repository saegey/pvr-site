This one was special for us: **the debut of our new custom speakers** — Baltic birch cabinets, waveguide-loaded highs, and built with a ridiculous amount of care by **[Will Jennings](https://www.instagram.com/will_is_biking/)**. Will was there in person to hear them in the wild, in a real room, with a real crowd. No lab conditions. Just bodies, chatter, clinking glasses, and low-end pressure.

<ShowCarousel />

We hit a couple snags: a flaky Technics dropped a channel for three songs and a cartridge head came loose mid-set. We fixed it on the fly and didn’t let it derail the night. Lesson learned: keep your gear dialed.

Musically, this set lives where we love to live: **tropical grooves, cumbia pressure, Latin psych, disco-boogie heat, and the weird left turns that make it feel alive**. From the deep, slow-build opener into the first wave of boogie, then straight into Amazonian cumbia and modern Colombian mutations — it was one long rising line.

Huge thanks to **Andrew** and everyone at Human People for having us and making the night feel easy. This one felt like a real milestone: new sound, full room, and the kind of vibe that reminds us why we do this.

See you at the next one. ✌️
